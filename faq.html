Before you order

Frequently asked questions

A few things worth knowing about setup, shipping, and returns.

How do I set it to my town?

Every Wave Clock ships with the same board — you pick your town from the settings menu right on the device after it arrives. Any of the 8 towns works, no need to specify anything at checkout.

How fast does it ship?

Orders usually ship within 4 days. From there it goes out UPS Ground, so add a few more days in transit depending on your location.

What's the return policy?

Free returns within 30 days of delivery — you just cover the cost of shipping it back.
