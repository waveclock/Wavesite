Setup help

Android didn't show the WiFi setup page?

Good news: this is a known quirk with how some Android phones handle this step, not anything wrong with your clock. Here's the fix.

Step 1

Open a browser

Once your phone is connected to the clock's WiFi network ("WaveClock-Setup"), open a web browser — Chrome, or whatever you normally use.

Step 2

Type in the address bar

Type this into the address bar:

http://192.168.4.1

Step 3

Press go

That should bring up the setup page directly, where you can pick your home WiFi network and enter your password.

A couple of things that trip people up:
  • Make sure it's http:// and not https:// — some phones auto-add the "s" and the page won't load that way.
  • If your phone shows a pop-up saying something like "WiFi has no internet, stay connected?" — say yes, that's expected.

Give that a try and see if it gets you through!
